BAIUST Computer Club (BCC)

BAIUST Computer Club, known as BCC, is one of the pioneer clubs of Bangladesh Army International University of Science & Technology, Cumilla Cantonment, Cumilla, Bangladesh.

It's the leading, inspiring, and resourceful co-curricular activities club, started its initiation in 2015, for ensuring proper development in the information technology sector through its affiliates.
